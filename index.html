Dr. Sharma, trained as an ecologist, has a diverse educational background, starting with a Bachelor of Science in Ecology. He furthered his expertise by earning a Master of Science in Environment Systems, specializing in environmental systems and modeling. His academic journey culminated in a Doctor of Science in Earth Sciences/Biosphere, where he developed expertise in satellite remote sensing and proximity sensing. Dr. Sharma's extensive academic experiences have honed his proficiency, particularly in extracting biophysical parameters from the Earth's surface using satellite remote sensing and applying large-scale geospatial applications with machine learning. He is recognized for his deep understanding of ecological systems and their dynamics through the analysis of data from multiple satellite technologies. His work addresses critical ecological challenges and environmental pressures faced by humanity.
